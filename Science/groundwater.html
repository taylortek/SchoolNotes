Groundwater

  • Groundwater is water that is found in the pores and spaces between soil particles.
  • Groundwater contains about 0.3 percent of the total water on earth

Groundwater Storage

  • After a rain event, water can runoff, evaporate or *infiltrate* into the ground

Porosity

  • The amount of pore space in a material

Permeability

  • The ability of a material to let water pass through it
