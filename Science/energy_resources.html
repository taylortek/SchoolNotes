Traditional Energy Sources

Renewable Vs. Nonrenewable

  • Renewable can be replenished over a relatively short period of time
    • Examples: Solar, Wood, Geothermal
  • Nonrenewable sources can take millions of years to form and accumulate
    • Examples: Fossil fuels (oil, coal, natural gas), Nuclear

Impacts of Mining Coal

  • Subsurface Mining
    • fire, collapse, explosion
    • black lung disease
    • land subsidence (like a sinkhole)
    • acid mine drainage into surface and groundwater
